# Meezo Driver Privacy Policy
*Effective Date: April 16, 2026*

This Driver Privacy Policy explains how Meezo collects, uses, stores, and protects information related to drivers who use the Meezo platform. By using the Meezo Driver App, the driver agrees to the practices described in this policy.

Meezo collects driver information to manage driver accounts, support order delivery, show maps and live locations, track earnings and settlements, and maintain operational, security, and payment records.

## 1. Information We Collect
Meezo may collect and store the following driver information:
- Full name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Date of birth
- Home address
- NIC number
- Bank account details
- License number and license-related details
- Profile photo
- Email and password login information

## 2. Operational and Driver Activity Information
Meezo may also collect driver-related business and activity data such as:
- Live and background location information
- Vehicle details
- Order history
- Earnings records
- Cash collected from customers
- Settlement history
- Login-related activity and account use records

## 3. Information Visible to Drivers
To complete deliveries and support delivery operations, drivers may be able to view:
- Customer name
- Customer phone number
- Customer address
- Customer live location where applicable
- Order details
- Earnings information
- Wallet or settlement-related details

## 4. How We Use Driver Information
Meezo may use driver information for the following purposes:
- To create and manage driver accounts
- To authenticate login using email and password
- To assign and coordinate deliveries
- To show maps, addresses, and location-related delivery information
- To track order completion and delivery history
- To manage earnings, wallet records, cash collection, and settlements
- To support customer communication for valid delivery-related reasons
- To maintain safety, platform monitoring, and service reliability

## 5. Location Information
Meezo collects, processes, and stores driver live and background location data to support order assignment, navigation, delivery tracking, customer updates, and operational monitoring. 

**Google Play Store Policy Compliance Disclosure:**
The Meezo Driver app collects location data to track active deliveries and share live routes with customers, **even when the app is closed, in the background, or not in use**. Background location collection is active only when:
- The driver is logged into the app and has toggled their status to "Online".
- The driver has an active delivery assigned and is on the way to the restaurant or customer.

Location data is used solely for legitimate delivery, route optimization, and safety monitoring purposes and is not shared with any third-party advertising partners.

## 6. Cash Collection and Settlement Information
If a driver collects money from a customer, Meezo may record the amount collected, settlement details, submission history, pending balances, and related payment information.

### Additional Identity and Payment Information
Meezo may collect and store additional identity and payment-related information such as date of birth, home address, NIC number, and bank account details where required for driver verification, compliance, settlement processing, payment management, or operational record keeping.

## 7. Data Sharing
Driver information may be shared only when necessary, including:
- With managers for driver management, settlement review, safety, and operational oversight
- With authorized support staff for account or delivery issue resolution
- With customers only as needed for delivery completion, such as driver name or contact-related information
- When required by law or to protect platform safety, business rights, or service operations

## 8. Driver Privacy Responsibilities
Drivers must follow these privacy responsibilities:
- Customer data must not be misused
- Customer contact details must be used only for delivery purposes
- Drivers must not request or collect extra unauthorized payment
- Visible data must be used only for official platform and delivery work

## 9. Data Protection
Meezo takes reasonable technical and operational steps to protect driver data from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system can guarantee complete security.

## 10. Data Retention
Meezo may retain driver account records, order history, cash collection records, settlement records, and related activity information for operational, security, legal, and support purposes.

## 11. Policy Updates
Meezo may update this Driver Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the driver platform after updates means acceptance of the revised policy.
